Special Characters

These are special characters in the context of HTML because they have special meaning in HTML

  1. Copyright : ©
  2. Trademark : ™
  3. Greater than : > or >
  4. Lesser than: <
  5. Registered Symbol : ®
  6. Left single quote : ‘
  7. Right single quote : ’
  8. Normal single quote : ' (This doesn't show the direction of the quote, whether it is opening quote or closing quote)
  9. Right double quote : ”
  10. Left double quote : “
  11. Normal double quote : " (Doesn't show whether it is opening or closing quote)
